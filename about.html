Bella Demhat aka Ms.K

Her story is told through many forms of art, Bella aka Ms.K is a performer, DJ and foremost she makes white canvas come alive with tasteful lines and color combinations.

Her signature style is defined by dark lines in contrast to rich colors, she portraits faces and bodies beyond gender borders.

DJ Ms.K

Bella is a transgender rights activist from Kurdistan on her quest to smash oppression and dance her way to freedom!

As a resident of PlayingFavorites Ms.K is a well-known DJ in Stockholm with her fast techno sounds from the queer bipoc music scene.
